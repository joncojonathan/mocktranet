Company policies

We're a fairly open and relaxed company, however, we do ask all staff to adhere to these policies. This is to make working here a good experience for all of the team.

Acceptable Internet usage

The Internet is an incredibly powerful tool but not everything on the Internet is appropriate in the workplace. On the whole, we trust our employees to make common sense judgments when it comes to what is acceptable. That said, here are some guidelines:

  • Pornography may not be accessed from the office network, or when on a work device away from the office
  • You may not search for terrorist or extremist materials, or materials which encourage hateful behaviour
  • During breaks you may check your personal email, read the news, etc.
  • You may not download programs or scripts from the Internet. If in doubt, consult ICT

Annual leave

To book annual leave you must place confirm the date(s) with your manager. Annual leave should then be clearly marked in your diary. It is expected that an out of office automatic response is enabled on your email.
